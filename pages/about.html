ANDERSON joseph

In my creative practice I take a lot of inspiration from the ethos of avant-garde cinema. I am particularly interested in exploring the space between photography and film, outlets such as durational film, long exposure photography, and early animation. In my practice I often mix analog methods with digital tools and I view my process as collaborative one with technology. I welcome technological hurdles and disruptions in my work. I have an inherent need to creatively solve problems which often leads me down daring paths. I feel a lot of pleasure in visual chaos and I want to match, smash, and stack these fleeting sensations. Not with the goal of deconstructing it but rather to make it all sing louder.
